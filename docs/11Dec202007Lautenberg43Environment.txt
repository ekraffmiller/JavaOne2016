  Lautenberg Statement 
After Senate Vote on 'FRESH' Act N.J. Sen. to continue fight to reform 'nation's flawed farm policy' 
Tuesday, December 11, 2007 WASHINGTON, D.C. U.S. Sen. Frank 
R. Lautenberg (D-NJ) issued the following statement after s vote on the FRESH Act, legislation 
he offered with Sen. Richard G. Lugar (R-IN) to reform our nations farm policy: This fight is not 
over. I will continue to work to reform our nations flawed farm policy that shortchanges New Jersey 
and sends too much money to too few farms. Thats why we are fighting to changethis system and we will 
continue that effort in the years to come.
