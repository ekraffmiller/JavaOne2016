 
Remarks on the Funeral of the Late Coretta Scott King  
After attending 's funeral for Coretta Scott King at the New Birth Missionary Baptist Church 
in Lithonia, Georgia United States Senator Frank R. Lautenberg issued the following statement. 
Senator Robert Menendez also attended the ceremony as part of a Congressional delegation. "It 
was a service of great dignity and inspiration. I left not just with memories but with a message to 
carry on. Coretta Scott King and Martin Luther King may have both departed this world, but they have 
set an example for all of us to follow," said Senator Lautenberg.
